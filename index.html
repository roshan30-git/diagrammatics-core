System Features

Designed for heavy-STEM processing and cognitive load reduction.

Visual Intelligence

Plain text is a failure state. We enforce diagrammatic outputs. From Mermaid.js flows to side-by-side comparison cards, our Visual Solver ensures you see the answer, not just read it.

Persistent Study Memory

Your session never starts from zero. With our local sqlite-vec + mem0 backend, your past sessions, topics, language preferences, and Quick Recall cards accumulate over your semester.

Grounded Retrieval

No hallucinations. We parse your syllabus, PDFs, and OCR scans locally. Your queries are answered strictly based on the context you provide through the MVP Local RAG system.
