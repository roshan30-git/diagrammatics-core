Agent Swarm Orchestration

Instead of one chatbot, we deploy a team. Each agent has its own capabilities and persona:

  • Mentor Agent: Handles guidance, tone control, and study schedules.
  • Solver Agent: Specialized in multi-part STEM assignments and LaTeX injection.
  • Hunter Agent: Background autonomous scraping of past papers using Scrapling.

Agents debate in a shared context window to ensure grounded, zero-hallucination results.

Tree-RAG: Hierarchical Memory

Flat vector search fails for academia. We index your life in a hierarchy:

SEMESTER
SUBJECT
TOPIC (SESSIONS)

This allows the AI to understand that a question about "Fourier Transforms" in a DSP session should prioritize your DSP class notes over a generic calculus summary.

Integrated Note Saving

A completely local Zettelkasten note-taking system. No need for external apps like Obsidian or Notion. Your notes are saved locally, auto-tagged by the Swarm, and instantly injected into the Tree-RAG memory.

  • Bilingual OCR (English & Local Scripts).
  • Press a hotkey to instantly capture screen clips into your notes.

Visual Synthesis Engine (VSE)

Diagrammatics agents don't just "talk"—they build. The VSE allows agents to natively generate:

  • Mermaid.js: For flowcharts and system diagrams.
  • tldraw: For infinite whiteboard sketches.
  • Plotly.js: For 3D math and scientific surfaces.
  • SchemDraw: Auto-generate complex electrical circuits.
  • Presentation Mode: Auto-compile visual outputs into ready-to-present PPT slides.

GTU Hunter (Scrapling Sidecar)

A specialized background process using Python's Scrapling library. It automatically penetrates university portals and indexes up to 10 years of PYQs (Previous Year Questions) directly into your local database.

The Swarm uses this indexed data to generate highly accurate Mock Exams based on historical professor patterns.

NotebookLM Connect

Seamlessly integrate with Google's NotebookLM via MCP (Model Context Protocol). Sync your local folders directly to NotebookLM to generate highly grounded Audio Overviews and podcasts of your lectures, without manually uploading PDFs every time.

Pro Academic Export

When the assignment is solved, you need to submit it. Our export engine uses Tauri headless WebViews to render your glassmorphic UI directly into Pixel-Perfect PDFs.

  • Preserves KaTeX equations and advanced symbols.
  • Outputs strict markdown for GitHub/blog posting.

Custom Plugins & Skills (Future)

The "Nexus Network" will introduce a community-driven Agent Marketplace. Since we rely on the open Model Context Protocol (MCP), users can write their own Python skills (e.g., "Auto-CAD Plugin", "MATLAB interface") and share them with other engineering students.
