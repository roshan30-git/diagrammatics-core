The Genesis: Why We Built This

The failure of generic AI in serious academia.

C:\DOCS\The_Problem.txt

The modern engineering student is drowning in context, but starving for structure.

When you ask a generic AI (like ChatGPT) to solve a 7-mark GTU university question, it fails. It hallucinates syllabus bounds, ignores local university marking schemes, and outputs paragraphs of text when what you actually need is a SchemDraw circuit diagram or a Mermaid flowchart.

Worse, your intellectual property—your assignments, your research, your late-night derivation attempts—are sent to the cloud to train global models.

The Diagrammatics Solution

We built a Local-First Mainframe. It doesn't just chat; it orchestrates. Using AgentScope, we deploy a "Swarm" of specialized agents right on your local machine. They hunt for past papers, solve complex multi-part math, and automatically inject high-fidelity SVG diagrams into your workflow. Zero telemetry. Infinite recall.
